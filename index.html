Know Your Aadhaar for Scholarship DBT

Ensure your bank account is Aadhaar-seeded and DBT-enabled to receive scholarships on time.

Understand the Difference Enable DBT Now
Important: Scholarships under Pre-Matric and Post-Matric schemes are paid ONLY via DBT into a DBT-enabled Aadhaar-seeded bank account.

Aadhaar-Linked vs DBT-Enabled Aadhaar-Seeded

Aadhaar-Linked Bank Account

  • Bank has your Aadhaar for KYC/records.
  • Does NOT guarantee direct government payments.
  • May still require seeding and DBT activation.

DBT-Enabled Aadhaar-Seeded Account

  • Aadhaar is seeded in bank‚Äôs NPCI mapper.
  • DBT enabled for receiving government benefits.
  • Required for scholarship disbursement.

How to Enable DBT

  1. Ensure Aadhaar is active and updated

    Update mobile/email/biometrics at the nearest Aadhaar Seva Kendra if needed.

    Visit UIDAI

  2. Seed Aadhaar with your bank account

    Use your bank‚Äôs Aadhaar seeding options (branch, ATM, mobile/online banking).

  3. Ask bank to enable DBT (NPCI mapping)

    Confirm NPCI mapper shows your account as primary for DBT.

    Check Mapper

  4. Verify on NSP during application

    Ensure the same Aadhaar and bank details are used on the National Scholarship Portal.
